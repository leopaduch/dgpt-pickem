DGPT MPO Standings
How It Works
Each week, Leo and Joe each rank 8 MPO players as their draft pool. The app resolves picks in snake draft order — whoever went second last week goes first this week. Your top available player gets drafted to your team.

Each player also ranks 6 top-10 DGPT players to assign to their opponent. The opponent's highest-ranked available player is forced onto their roster.

Draft submissions open Monday and close Wednesday at midnight each tournament week.
Scoring
Each week is worth 20 points. If one of your 3 players wins the tournament outright, you collect the points. If no pick wins, the week pushes and points roll over to the following week, adding to the next week's pot.
Snake Draft Order
Week 1: Joe picks first. The order flips every week — whoever went first last week goes second next week.
